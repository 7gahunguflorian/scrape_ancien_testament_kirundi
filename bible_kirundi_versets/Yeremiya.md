**15.** Muri iyo misi no muri ico gihe nzonagishiriza Dawidi umunago ugororotse , kandi azotuma mu gihugu harangwa ubutungane n’ubugororotsi .

**16.** Muri iyo misi Ubuyuda buzokizwa na Yeruzalemu yibere mu mutekano . Ico gisagara kizokwitwa: Yehova ni we bugororotsi bwacu .”»

**17.** «Kuko ibi ari vyo Yehova avuze: “Mu muryango wa Dawidi ntihazokwigera habura uwicara ku ntebe y’ubwami ya Isirayeli .

**18.** No mu baherezi b’Abalewi ntihazokwigera habura umugabo ahagarara imbere yanje ngo ashikane amashikanwa aturirwa uko yakabaye, ngo aturire amashikanwa y’intete yongere ashikane ibimazi.”»

**19.** Yehova yongera kuvugana na Yeremiya ati:

**20.** «Ibi ni vyo Yehova avuze: “Mbega mwoshobora guhagarika isezerano nagize rijanye n’umurango n’isezerano nagize rijanye n’ijoro, mukabuza umurango n’ijoro kuza ku mwanya wavyo ?

**21.** Nimba vyoshoboka, n’isezerano nagiranye n’umusavyi wanje Dawidi ryoshobora rero guhagarikwa , ku buryo ataronka umuhungu yicara ku ntebe yiwe y’ubwami ngo aganze . N’isezerano nagiranye n’abaherezi b’Abalewi bankorera na ryo nyene ryohagarikwa.

**22.** Nk’uko ingabo zo kw’ijuru zidashobora guharurwa, canke ngo umusenyi wo ku kiyaga upimwe, ni ko nzogwiza uruvyaro rw’umusavyi wanje Dawidi n’Abalewi bankorera.”»

**23.** Yehova yongera kandi kuvugana na Yeremiya ati:

**24.** «Mbega ntiwumvise ivyo aba bantu bavuga? Bavuga ngo: “Yehova azoheba ya miryango ibiri yatora.” Baguma bagaya abantu banje, bakabona ko batakiri ihanga.

**25.** Yehova avuze ati: “Nk’uko nyene nashinze isezerano rijanye n’umurango be n’ijoro , ngashinga n’amategeko agenga ijuru n’isi ,

**26.** ni ko nyene ntazokwigera mpeba uruvyaro rwa Yakobo n’urw’umusavyi wanje Dawidi, ngo ndeke gutora mu ruvyaro rwiwe abatware bo kuganza abakomoka kuri Aburahamu , Izahaki na Yakobo. Kuko nzokoranya abantu babo bagizwe imbohe ndabagarukane, kandi nzobumvira akagongwe .”»

**33.** Yehova avugana na Yeremiya ubugira kabiri, igihe yari agipfungiwe mu kigo c’abarinzi , amubwira ati:

**2.** «Ibi ni vyo Yehova Imana ya Isirayeli avuze: “Genda uvugane na Zedekiya umwami w’Ubuyuda, umubwire uti: ‘Yehova avuze ati: “Ngira ntange iki gisagara ku mwami wa Babiloni, kandi azogiturira .

**3.** Ntuzomucarukana kuko uzofatwa ata kabuza bakagushikana ku mwami wa Babiloni . Uzomubona amaso mu yandi, muvugane imbonankubone, kandi uzoja Babiloni .”

**4.** Ariko rero Zedekiya mwami w’Ubuyuda, niwumve ijambo rya Yehova: “Yehova avuze ku bikwerekeye ati: ‘Ntuzokwicishwa inkota.

**5.** Uzokwipfira amahoro, kandi nibaguhamba, bazoturira imibavu nk’uko babigiriye ba sogokuruza, abami ba kera bakwitangiye imbere. Bazokuririra bavuga bati: “Yoo, databuja!” Ivyo bizoba kuko “ari jewe ndabivuze”, ni ko Yehova avuze.’”’”»

**6.** Nuko umuhanuzi Yeremiya abwira Zedekiya umwami w’Ubuyuda ayo majambo yose, ayamubwirira Yeruzalemu.

**7.** Ico gihe ingabo z’umwami wa Babiloni zariko zirwanya Yeruzalemu n’ibisagara vyose vy’Ubuyuda vyari bisigaye , ni ukuvuga Lakishi na Azeka . Mu bisagara vyo mu Buyuda bikingijwe ibihome, ni vyo vyonyene vyari bisigaye.

**8.** Yehova yaravuganye na Yeremiya inyuma y’aho umwami Zedekiya amariye kugirana isezerano n’abantu bose bari Yeruzalemu kugira ngo bahe umwidegemvyo abashumba .

**9.** Umwe wese yategerezwa kurekura umushumba canke umushumbakazi wiwe w’Umuheburayo, kugira ntihagire uwubandanya gukoresha Umuyuda mwenewabo nk’umushumba.

**10.** Nuko abatware bose n’abantu bose baragamburuka. Bari basezeranye yuko umwe wese yorekuye abashumba n’abashumbakazi biwe ntibabe bakibashumbira. Baragamburutse, barabarekura.

**11.** Mugabo mu nyuma, bagarukana abashumba n’abashumbakazi bari barekuye, basubira kubagira abashumba ku nguvu.

**12.** Nuko Yehova avugana na Yeremiya. Yehova avuga ati:

**13.** «Ibi ni vyo Yehova Imana ya Isirayeli avuze: “Naragiranye isezerano na ba sogokuruza banyu ku musi nabakura mu gihugu ca Misiri aho mwari abashumba , mvuga nti:

**14.** ‘Imyaka indwi niyarangira, umwe wese muri mwebwe aze arekure Umuheburayo mwenewabo azoba yaguze akamukorera imyaka itandatu. Aze amurekure yidegemvye .’ Mugabo ba sogokuruza banyu ntibanyumvirije canke ngo bantege amatwi.

**15.** Namwe muherutse kwisubirako mukora ivyo mbona ko bigororotse, muha benewanyu umwidegemvyo, mugira n’isezerano imbere yanje mu nzu yitirirwa izina ryanje.

**16.** Mugabo mwaciye mwongera kwisubirako muhumanya izina ryanje , mugarukana abashumba banyu n’abashumbakazi banyu mwari mwarekuye bakaja iyo bashaka. Mwasubiye kubagira abashumba ku nguvu.”

**17.** Yehova avuze rero ati: “Ntimwangamburukiye igihe mwaha umwidegemvyo benewanyu na bagenzi banyu . Nanje rero ngira ndabahe umwidegemvyo wo kwicwa n’inkota, ikiza n’inzara ”, ni ko Yehova avuze, “kandi nzotuma mucika akajoreza imbere y’ubwami bwose bwo kw’isi .

**18.** Ibi ni vyo bizoshikira abarenze kw’isezerano ryanje, ntibashitse ibiri mw’isezerano bagize imbere yanje igihe bacamwo kubiri inyana, bagaca hagati y’ibihimba vyayo ,

**19.** ni ukuvuga abatware bo mu Buyuda, abatware b’i Yeruzalemu, abakozi bo ku kirimba, abaherezi n’abanyagihugu bose baciye hagati y’ibihimba vy’iyo nyana:

**20.** nzobatanga ku bansi babo no ku barondera kubica, kandi imivyimba yabo izoribwa n’inyoni zo mu kirere n’ibikoko vyo kw’isi .

**21.** Zedekiya umwami w’Ubuyuda n’abatware biwe, nzobatanga ku bansi babo no ku barondera kubica no ku ngabo z’umwami wa Babiloni ziriko zirava iwanyu .”

**22.** Yehova avuze ati: “Ngira ntange itegeko, ndabagarukane muri iki gisagara, bakirwanye, bagifate maze bagiturire . Nzotuma ibisagara vyo mu Buyuda bicika agahinga, bisigare ata wubibamwo .”»

**34.** Yehova yaravuganye na Yeremiya, igihe Nebukadinezari umwami wa Babiloni n’ingabo ziwe zose n’ubwami bwose aganza bwo kw’isi, hamwe n’amahanga yose, bariko barwanya Yeruzalemu n’ibisagara vyose bihakikuje . Yavuze ati:

**2.** «Ja kuraba abo mu muryango w’Abarekabu uvugane na bo, maze ubazane mu nzu ya Yehova, muri bumwe mu buriro bwaho, ubahe umuvinyu banywe.»

**3.** Ntora rero Yazaniya mwene Yeremiya, Yeremiya mwene Habaziniya, na benewabo, n’abahungu biwe bose n’umuryango wose w’Abarekabu,

**4.** ndabazana mu nzu ya Yehova. Nabatwaye mu buriro bwa bene Hanane mwene Igidaliya, umuntu w’Imana y’ukuri, iruhande y’uburiro bw’abatware . Uburiro bw’abatware bwari hejuru y’uburiro bwa Mayaseya mwene Shalumu umurinzi w’umuryango.

**5.** Maze ntereka imbere y’abagabo bo mu muryango w’Abarekabu ibikombe n’ibindi banyweramwo vyuzuye umuvinyu, hanyuma ndababwira nti: «Nimunywe umuvinyu.»

**6.** Mugabo bavuga bati: «Nta muvinyu tunywa, kuko Yehonadabu mwene Rekabu, sogokuruza wacu, yadutegetse ati: “Ntimuze mwigere munywa umuvinyu, yaba mwebwe canke abahungu banyu.

**7.** Kandi ntimuze mwubake inzu canke ngo mubibe imbuto, canke ngo mutere umurima w’imizabibu canke ngo muwironse. Mukwiye nyabuna kuguma muba mu mahema kugira ngo murambire mu gihugu mubayemwo nk’abanyamahanga.”

**8.** Turabandanya rero kugamburuka ivyo Yehonadabu mwene Rekabu sogokuruza wacu yadutegetse vyose, mu kutigera tunywa umuvinyu, yaba twebwe, abagore bacu, abahungu bacu canke abakobwa bacu.

**9.** Nta n’amazu yo kubamwo twubaka, kandi nta mirima y’imizabibu dufise canke amatongo canke imbuto.

**10.** Tubandanya kuba mu mahema no kugamburuka ivyo sogokuruza wacu Yehonadabu yadutegetse vyose.

**11.** Mugabo igihe Nebukadinezari umwami wa Babiloni yaza gutera igihugu , twibwiye duti: “Reka tuje Yeruzalemu duhunge ingabo z’Abakaludaya n’Abasiriya.” Ni yo mpamvu ubu twibera Yeruzalemu.»

**12.** Maze Yehova abwira Yeremiya ati:

**13.** «Ibi ni vyo Yehova Nyeningabo, Imana ya Isirayeli, avuze: “Genda ubwire abagabo bo mu Buyuda n’ababa Yeruzalemu uti: ‘Mbega sinaguma ndabaremesha ngo mugamburuke ivyo mvuga ?’, ni ko Yehova avuze.

**14.** ‘Yehonadabu mwene Rekabu yategetse abamukomokako kutanywa umuvinyu, kandi barabikurikije mu kutanywa umuvinyu gushika n’uyu musi, bagamburuka itegeko rya sekuruza wabo . Ariko jewe navuganye namwe incuro n’izindi mugabo ntimwangamburukiye .

**15.** Nagumye ndabatumako abasavyi banje bose b’abahanuzi incuro n’izindi , nkavuga nti: “Ndabasavye, umwe wese ahindukire ahebe ingeso ziwe mbi , akore ibigororotse. Ntimukurikire izindi mana kandi ntimuzisenge . Muzoheza mubandanye kuba mu gihugu nabahaye mwe na ba sogokuruza banyu .” Ariko ntimwateze ugutwi canke ngo munyumvirize.

**16.** Abakomoka kuri Yehonadabu mwene Rekabu barakurikije itegeko rya sekuruza wabo , ariko aba bantu bo ntibanyumvirije.’”»

**17.** «Yehova, Imana Nyeningabo, Imana ya Isirayeli, avuze rero ati: “Ngira nzane ku Buyuda no ku baba Yeruzalemu bose ivyago vyose nababwiye ko bizobashikira , kuko navuganye na bo mugabo ntibumviriza, nkaguma ndabahamagara mugabo ntibitabe .”»

**18.** Yeremiya abwira abo mu muryango w’Abarekabu ati: «Yehova Nyeningabo, Imana ya Isirayeli, avuze ati: “Ko mwagamburutse itegeko Yehonadabu sogokuruza wanyu yabahaye, mukabandanya gukurikiza ivyo yabategetse vyose, mugakora neza na neza ivyo yabategetse,

**19.** Yehova Nyeningabo, Imana ya Isirayeli, avuze rero ati: ‘Mu bakomoka kuri Yehonadabu mwene Rekabu ntihazokwigera habura uwukorera imbere yanje.’”»

**35.** Yehova yavuganye na Yeremiya mu gihe ca Yehoyakimu mwene Yosiya, umwami w’Ubuyuda, amubwira ati:

**2.** «Tora umuzingo uwandikemwo amajambo yose nakubwiye yo kuburira Isirayeli n’Ubuyuda n’amahanga yose . Nayakubwiye kuva umusi wa mbere navuganye nawe mu gihe ca Yosiya gushika n’uyu musi .

**3.** Abo mu Buyuda nibumva ivyago vyose ngira ndabateze, hari aho bohindukira bagaheba ingeso zabo mbi, nanje nkababarira ikosa ryabo n’igicumuro cabo .»

**4.** Yeremiya ahamagara rero Baruki mwene Neriya. Maze Yeremiya amubwira ivyo Yehova yamubariye vyose, Baruki na we avyandika mu muzingo .

**5.** Yeremiya aca ategeka Baruki ati: «Jewe ndapfunzwe, sinshobora kwinjira mu nzu ya Yehova.

**6.** Ni wewe rero utegerezwa kwinjirayo, ugasoma n’ijwi ryumvikana amajambo ya Yehova nakubwiye ngo wandike muri uyo muzingo. Uyasomere mu nzu ya Yehova abantu bumva, ku musi wo kwisonzesha. Bizotuma abantu bose bo mu Buyuda bazoba baje bavuye mu bisagara vyabo bayumva.

**7.** Hari aho Yehova yokwumva ugutakamba kwabo, bagahindukira, umwe wese agaheba ingeso ziwe mbi, kuko Yehova yavuze ko afitiye aba bantu ishavu ryinshi n’uburake bwinshi.»

**8.** Nuko Baruki mwene Neriya akora ivyo umuhanuzi Yeremiya yari yamutegetse vyose. Asomera mu nzu ya Yehova n’ijwi ryumvikana amajambo ya Yehova yari muri wa muzingo .

**9.** Nuko mu mwaka ugira gatanu w’intwaro ya Yehoyakimu mwene Yosiya, umwami w’Ubuyuda, mu kwezi kugira icenda, abantu bose b’i Yeruzalemu n’abantu bose baje Yeruzalemu bavuye mu bisagara vy’Ubuyuda batangaza umusi wo kwisonzesha imbere ya Yehova .

**10.** Maze Baruki asomera mu nzu ya Yehova n’ijwi ryumvikana amajambo ya Yeremiya yari muri wa muzingo . Yayasomeye mu cumba ca Gemariya mwene Shafani umwimuzi cari mu kigo co haruguru, hafi y’ubwinjiriro bw’irembo rishasha ry’inzu ya Yehova , ayasoma abantu bose bumva.

**11.** Mikaya mwene Gemariya, Gemariya mwene Shafani, yumvise amajambo yose ya Yehova yari muri nya muzingo ,

**12.** aca amanuka ku kirimba c’umwami mu cumba c’umunyamabanga. Abatware bose ni ho bari bicaye. Hari umunyamabanga Elishama , Delaya mwene Shemaya, Elinatani mwene Akibori , Gemariya mwene Shafani, Zedekiya mwene Hananiya n’abandi batware bose.

**13.** Nuko Mikaya ababwira amajambo yose yumvise igihe Baruki yasomera imbere y’abantu ibiri muri nya muzingo .

**14.** Maze abatware bose batuma Yehudi mwene Netaniya, Netaniya wa Shelemiya, Shelemiya wa Kushi, bamutuma kuri Baruki ngo amubwire ati: «Nuze uzanye uwo muzingo wasomeye imbere y’abantu.» Baruki mwene Neriya aca atora nya muzingo, aja iyo bari.

**15.** Maze bamubwira bati: «Turagusavye wicare udusomere uwo muzingo n’ijwi ryumvikana.» Nuko Baruki arawubasomera.

**16.** Bakimara kwumva ayo majambo yose, barabana bafise ubwoba maze babwira Baruki bati: «Uko biri kwose dutegerezwa kubwira umwami ayo majambo yose.»

**17.** Nuko babaza Baruki bati: «Turagusavye utubwire ukuntu wanditse ayo majambo yose. Ni Yeremiya yayakubwiye?»

**18.** Baruki abishura ati: «Ego. Yarambwiye ayo majambo yose, nanje ndayandikisha irangi muri uyu muzingo .»

**19.** Abo batware baca babwira Baruki bati: «Mwe na Yeremiya genda mwinyegeze, ntihagire n’umwe mubwira aho muri .»

**20.** Maze bafata nya muzingo bawushira mu cumba c’umunyamabanga Elishama, hanyuma baja aho umwami ari mu kigo bamubwira ivyo bumvise vyose.

**21.** Nuko umwami atuma Yehudi kuzana nya muzingo, maze aja kuwutora mu cumba c’umunyamabanga Elishama. Yehudi atangura kuwusomera imbere y’umwami n’abatware bose bari bahagaze iruhande y’umwami.

**22.** Hari mu kwezi kugira icenda umwami yicaye mu nzu yo mu rushana, imbabura iriko iraka imbere yiwe.

**23.** Yehudi amaze gusoma nk’inkingi zitatu canke zine, umwami yaca atabura ico gice akoresheje imbugita y’umunyamabanga, akagiterera mu muriro wari muri nya mbabura gushika uwo muzingo wose urangiriye muri uwo muriro.

**24.** Ntibagize ubwoba. Kandi yaba umwami canke abasuku biwe bose bumvise ayo majambo yose, nta witaburiyeko impuzu.

**25.** Naho Elinatani , Delaya na Gemariya batakambiye umwami ngo ntaturire uwo muzingo, ntiyabumvirije.

**26.** Vyongeye, umwami ategeka Yerameyeli umuhungu w’umwami, Seraya mwene Aziriyeli na Shelemiya mwene Abudeli gufata umunyamabanga Baruki n’umuhanuzi Yeremiya. Mugabo Yehova yagumye abahishije .

**27.** Inyuma y’aho umwami atuririye wa muzingo warimwo amajambo Baruki yanditse ayabwiwe na Yeremiya, Yehova yongera kubwira Yeremiya ati:

**28.** «Tora uwundi muzingo uwandikeko ya majambo yose yari mu muzingo wa mbere Yehoyakimu umwami w’Ubuyuda yaturiye .

**29.** Kandi uze ubwire Yehoyakimu umwami w’Ubuyuda uti: “Yehova avuze ati: ‘Waturiye uwo muzingo uvuga ngo: “Kubera iki wawanditsemwo ngo: ‘Umwami wa Babiloni azoza koko kandi azohonya iki gihugu, ntihagire umuntu canke igikoko asigazayo ’?”

**30.** Ibi rero ni vyo Yehova avuze kuri Yehoyakimu umwami w’Ubuyuda: “Ntazoronka uwicara ku ntebe y’ubwami ya Dawidi , kandi umuvyimba wiwe uzoguma wandagaye ukubitwe n’ubushuhe ku murango n’igikonyozi mw’ijoro .

**31.** We n’abamukomokako n’abasuku biwe nzobahanira ikosa ryabo, kandi bo n’ababa Yeruzalemu n’abagabo bo mu Buyuda nzobazanako ivyago vyose navuze ko nzobateza , kuko banse kwumva .”’”»

**32.** Nuko Yeremiya atora uwundi muzingo maze awuha umunyamabanga Baruki mwene Neriya. Aca awandikamwo amajambo yose yari mu muzingo Yehoyakimu umwami w’Ubuyuda yaturiye , ayabwiwe na Yeremiya. Harongewemwo n’ayandi majambo menshi nk’ayo.

**36.** Nuko mu mwaka ugira kane w’intwaro ya Yehoyakimu mwene Yosiya, umwami w’Ubuyuda, Yehova abwira Yeremiya ati:

**2.** Mugabo we n’abasuku biwe n’abanyagihugu ntibumvirije ivyo Yehova yavuze biciye ku muhanuzi Yeremiya.

**3.** Nuko umwami Zedekiya atuma Yehukali mwene Shelemiya na Zefaniya mwene Mayaseya umuherezi, ngo babwire umuhanuzi Yeremiya bati: «Ndakwinginze udusabire kuri Yehova Imana yacu.»

**4.** Yeremiya yaridegemvya mu bantu kuko batari bwamushire mw’ibohero .

**5.** Ingabo za Farawo zariko ziraza zivuye mu Misiri maze Abakaludaya bari bugarije Yeruzalemu baravyumva, baca barakinjura bareka gutera Yeruzalemu .

**6.** Maze Yehova abwira umuhanuzi Yeremiya ati:

**7.** «Ibi ni vyo Yehova Imana ya Isirayeli avuze: “Umwami w’Ubuyuda yabatumye kumbaza, genda mumubwire muti: ‘Umviriza! Ingabo za Farawo ziriko ziraza kubatabara, zizobwirizwa gusubira mu Misiri iyo zaje ziva .

**8.** Kandi Abakaludaya bazogaruka barwanye iki gisagara bagifate maze bagiturire .’

**9.** Yehova avuze ati: ‘Ntimwihende muvuga ngo: “Abakaludaya bazogenda ata kabuza, bareke kudutera.” Nta ho bazoja.

**10.** Naho mwokwica ingabo zose z’Abakaludaya ziriko zirabarwanya hagasigara gusa abakomeretse, bova mu mahema yabo bakaza guturira iki gisagara .’”»

**11.** Ingabo z’Abakaludaya zimaze gukinjura zikareka gutera Yeruzalemu kubera ingabo za Farawo ,

**12.** Yeremiya aca ava Yeruzalemu aja mu karere ka Benyamini gutora umugabane wiwe mu b’iwabo.

**13.** Mugabo umuhanuzi Yeremiya ageze kw’irembo rya Benyamini, umukuru w’abarinzi yitwa Iriya mwene Shelemiya, Shelemiya mwene Hananiya, aca aramufata amubwira ati: «Uriko uja kwishikana ku Bakaludaya!»

**14.** Mugabo Yeremiya amubwira ati: «Oya si vyo! Singiye kwishikana ku Bakaludaya.» Ariko Iriya yanka kumwumviriza, aca afata Yeremiya amushira abatware.

**15.** Abatware bashavurira cane Yeremiya , baramukubita maze bamupfungira mu nzu y’umunyamabanga Yehonatani, kuko yari isigaye ari ibohero.

**16.** Yeremiya yashizwe mu gasho , mu vyumba vyo mu kuzimu, agumayo imisi myinshi.

**17.** Nuko umwami Zedekiya amutumako, maze atangura kumubaza ibibazo mu mpisho mu nzu iwe . Amubaza ati: «Nta co Yehova yoba yakubwiye?» Yeremiya na we ati: «Kiriho.» Yongerako ati: «Uzotangwa ku mwami wa Babiloni !»

**18.** Yeremiya abwira kandi umwami Zedekiya ati: «Ni igiki nagucumuyeko wewe n’abasuku bawe n’aba bantu, catumye munta mw’ibohero?

**19.** None bari hehe ba bahanuzi banyu bababwira ngo: “Umwami wa Babiloni ntazoza kubatera no gutera iki gihugu ”?

**20.** Ndakwinginze rero wumvirize databuja, mwami wanje! Ndakwinginze wumve ugutakamba kwanje. Ntunsubize mu nzu y’umunyamabanga Yehonatani , kugira simpfireyo .»

**21.** Umwami Zedekiya aca ategeka ko Yeremiya acungirwa mu kigo c’abarinzi . Buri musi bamuha umukate w’umuzingi uvuye kw’ibarabara ry’abotsa imikate , gushika imikate yose iheze mu gisagara . Yeremiya aguma mu kigo c’abarinzi.

**37.** Nuko umwami Zedekiya mwene Yosiya atangura kuganza mu kibanza ca Koniya mwene Yehoyakimu, kuko Nebukadinezari umwami wa Babiloni yari yamugize umwami w’Ubuyuda .

**2.** «Ibi ni vyo Yehova avuze: “Uwuzoguma muri iki gisagara azokwicwa n’inkota, inzara n’ikiza . Mugabo uwuzokwishikana ku Bakaludaya azobaho. Azokiza ubuzima bwiwe abeho .”

**3.** Yehova avuze ati: “Iki gisagara kizotangwa ata kabuza ku ngabo z’umwami wa Babiloni, agifate .”»

**4.** Abatware babwira umwami bati: «Turagusavye uyu mugabo yicwe , kuko mu kuvuga ivyo ariko aca intege abasoda basigaye muri iki gisagara n’abantu bose. Kuko uyu mugabo ataronderera amahoro aba bantu, ahubwo abaronderera ivyago.»

**5.** Umwami Zedekiya abishura ati: «Ehe ari mu maboko yanyu, kuko ata na kimwe umwami yobabuza.»
